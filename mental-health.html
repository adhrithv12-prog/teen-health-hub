Mental Health Matters

Support, stress help, and emotional wellbeing for teens

Home Symptoms Mental Health

Stress

Stress is a normal response to pressure from school, friends, or life changes.

Helpful tips: Take breaks, sleep well, and talk to someone you trust.

Anxiety

Anxiety can feel like constant worry or nervousness, even when there is no clear danger.

Helpful tips: Deep breathing, journaling, and limiting caffeine can help reduce symptoms.

Sleep Problems

Poor sleep can affect mood, focus, and energy levels.

Helpful tips: Avoid screens before bed and try to keep a consistent sleep schedule.

When to Ask for Help

It’s important to talk to someone if you feel overwhelmed, hopeless, or stuck for a long time.

Important: Reaching out to a trusted adult, teacher, or doctor is a strong and positive step.
