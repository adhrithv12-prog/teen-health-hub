Common Symptoms

Simple explanations for everyday health concerns

Headache

Headaches can be caused by stress, dehydration, lack of sleep, or screen time.

See a doctor if: It is severe, frequent, or comes with vision problems or vomiting.

Fever

A fever is often a sign your body is fighting an infection.

See a doctor if: The fever is above 103°F or lasts more than 3 days.

Stomach Pain

Stomach pain can come from digestion issues, stress, or mild infections.

See a doctor if: Pain is severe, persistent, or localized in one area.

Fatigue

Feeling tired can be caused by lack of sleep, stress, or poor diet.

See a doctor if: Fatigue lasts for weeks or affects daily life.
